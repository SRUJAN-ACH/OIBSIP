Thomas Alva Edison

The man who discovered the light bulb

Here's a time line of Thomas Alva Edison life:

  • 1847 - born, 11 February, Milan, Ohio
  • 1869 - Works on stock ticker and printing telegraph,New York
  • 1871 - Thomas Alva Edison marries Mary Stillwell
  • 1876 - Developed Carbon-reistance telephone transmitter
  • 1879 - Developed an Incandescent lamp.
  • 1883 - Discovers and experiments with electrical discharge inside lamp.
  • 1884 - Wife Mary dies.
  • 1886 - Marries Mina Miller
  • 1889-95 - Concentrated activity on electromagnetic ore-separation work, Ogdensburg, New Jersey and More work on phonograph and also Development of storage battery
  • 1923 - Attempts to find alternative sources for rubber
  • 1931 - Dies October 18, West Orange, New Jersey.
